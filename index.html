Our Vision

Aedile's main goal is to become a hub for developers and entrepreneurs to meet, share and collaborate around Internet Computer projects and any other innovative ideas ! Our primary objective is to build a tool to gather the community and facilitate funding.

As an investor, you will have the opportunity to discover a large variety of brand new open services and many other cool projects.
